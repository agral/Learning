Standard h1 to h6:

h1 heading

h2 heading

h3 heading

h4 heading

h5 heading
h6 heading


Other tags can also be styled with "h{1-6}" class, which will make it appear as the respective header-element.
Here is an example of a p tag with h2 class:

I am a <p> tag, but I look just like an <h2>.



This can also be applied to other header tags:

I am actually an <h1>, but I look just like an <h6> tag.
